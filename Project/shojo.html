Shojo

Shōjo anime is created for young girls and usually emphasizes emotions, relationships, and personal growth. These series often explore romance, friendship, and self-discovery, with expressive art styles and character-driven storytelling. Popular shōjo anime include Sailor Moon, Fruits Basket, Cardcaptor Sakura, and Ouran High School Host Club. Shōjo anime appeals to many viewers for its emotional depth and relatable characters.
