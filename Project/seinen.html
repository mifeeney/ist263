Seinen

Seinen anime is aimed at adult men and typically features more mature themes, complex narratives, and darker subject matter. These shows may include psychological elements, moral ambiguity, graphic violence, or social commentary. Popular examples of seinen anime include Attack on Titan, Berserk, Tokyo Ghoul, Ghost in the Shell, and Cowboy Bebop. Seinen anime is often praised for its storytelling depth and cinematic style.
