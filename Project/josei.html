What is Josei?

Josei anime focuses on adult womens experiences and presents realistic portrayals of relationships, careers, and personal struggles. These stories tend to be grounded and emotionally nuanced.

Nana

Nana follows two young women with the same name whose lives intertwine as they chase their dreams, face love and heartbreak, and navigate the challenges of friendship and adulthood.

Watch if you enjoy drama, romance, and stories about personal growth and complex relationships.

Available on: Hulu, Disney+

Paradise Kiss

Paradise Kiss follows Yukari “Caroline” Hayasaka, a high school student who becomes involved with a group of fashion design students, discovering love, ambition, and her own path in life.

Watch if you enjoy romance, coming-of-age stories, and the world of fashion and creativity.

Available on: Crunchyroll

Princess Jellyfish

Princess Jellyfish follows Tsukimi Kurashita, a socially awkward girl obsessed with jellyfish, as she forms an unlikely friendship with stylish outsider Kuranosuke, discovering confidence, love, and self-expression.

Watch if you enjoy comedy, romance, and stories about friendship and personal growth.

Available on: Crunchyroll
