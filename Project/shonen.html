What is Shōnen?

Shōnen anime is primarily aimed at young boys, but it has become one of the most widely enjoyed anime categories worldwide. These series often focus on action, competition, and personal growth, with strong themes of friendship, perseverance, and overcoming challenges.

Naruto

Naruto follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest leader of his village while overcoming challenges and dangerous enemies.

Watch if you enjoy action, adventure, and stories about friendship and perseverance.

Available on: Hulu, Disney+, Crunchyroll, Netflix

One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his crew of pirates as they search for the legendary treasure known as the One Piece, facing dangerous enemies and epic adventures along the way.

Watch if you enjoy action, adventure, and a fun, imaginative world.

Available on: Disney+, Crunchyroll, Netflix

Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who becomes a Hunter to find his missing father, embarking on dangerous adventures and meeting powerful friends and foes along the way.

Watch if you enjoy action, adventure, and intricate storylines.

Available on: Hulu, Crunchyroll, Netflix
