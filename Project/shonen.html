Shonen

Shōnen anime is targeted toward young boys and is one of the most popular and recognizable categories worldwide. These series often focus on action, adventure, competition, and themes like friendship, perseverance, and self-improvement. Well-known shōnen anime include Naruto, Dragon Ball Z, One Piece, My Hero Academia, and Demon Slayer. While aimed at younger audiences, shōnen anime is enjoyed by viewers of all ages due to its exciting stories and memorable characters.
